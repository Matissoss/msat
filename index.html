msat

Mateus' school administration tool

🦀 Made in Rust

msat and its components are made in Rust to ensure being lightweight, efficient, fast and memory-safe.

🎵 Self-hostable

msat was made to be self-hostable, meaning that you can host it on any hardware compatible with msat
